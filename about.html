About Michael Reyes

Author • Storyteller • World-builder of Panthers & Vampires.

Michael Reyes is a college student, creator, and storyteller who grew up around real life streets, music, and hustle — and poured all of it into a supernatural universe where witches, vampires, and werepanthers move just as grimy and emotional as real people.

Panthers & Vampires: Blue Moon is the first book in a larger saga: a blend of urban culture, dark fantasy, and generational trauma that stretches from Salem to the modern day.

Every character is built with intention — flawed, loud, scared, loyal, and dangerous. The goal isn’t just monsters and magic. It’s to tell stories about love, loss, family, and what you’re willing to do for the people you claim.

When he’s not writing, Michael is working on music, visuals, and building out Michael Reyes Media — a full ecosystem for the Panthers & Vampires world: books, audio, visuals, and merch.

Series Vision

  • Multiple-book arc (Blue Moon is Book One)
  • Strong focus on character growth & consequences
  • Mix of street drama, horror, and emotional beats
  • Built for readers who like dark, cinematic storytelling
