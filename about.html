About Michael Reyes

Author • Storyteller • Creator of Panthers & Vampires.

Michael Reyes is a college student, creator, and storyteller who grew up around real life streets, music, and hustle — and poured all of it into a supernatural universe where witches, vampires, and werepanthers move just as real and emotional as the people they come from.

Panthers & Vampires: Blue Moon is the first book in a larger saga — a blend of urban culture, dark fantasy, and generational trauma that stretches from Salem to the modern day.

His characters are flawed, loyal, dangerous, and full of heart. The goal isn’t just monsters and magic — it’s telling stories about love, pain, growth, and survival.

When he’s not writing, Michael is building music, visuals, and the entire creative ecosystem behind Michael Reyes Media.

Series Vision

  • Deep character development
  • Mix of horror, fantasy, & street realism
  • Raw, cinematic storytelling
