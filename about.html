ABOUT THE WORLD

Panthers & Vampires

A supernatural universe built from the ground up — where Panthers, Vampires, Witches, and ancient tribes collide in ways the streets have never seen.

This world blends modern culture, mythology, and raw emotion — creating a saga that spans bloodlines, betrayal, love, war, power, and destiny.
